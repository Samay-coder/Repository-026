Interesting facts about camels

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.


Interesting facts about chameleons

  • Unlike many lizards, chameleons can't regrow their tails.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.
  • Their tongues can be as twice as long as their bodies when stretched out to catch prey!


Interesting facts about pandas

  • An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  • Pandas are usually born in August.
  • At 5 months old, giant pandas learn how to climb - sometimes practicing by climbing on their mother.
